1. INTRODUCTION & CORE CONCEPT
=========================================================
What is Vortex? 
Vortex is an AI-powered adaptive study planner designed to eliminate student burnout and optimize learning efficiency. Instead of rigid, static timetables that students inevitably fail to follow, Vortex creates a dynamic, fluid study schedule that adapts to the student's actual cognitive state, performance, and attention span.

How it tackles the core question:
"Adaptive Study Planner Based on Learning Patterns: Create a system that adjusts study recommendations based on attention span, mistakes, and revision habits."

Vortex directly solves this by tracking:
- Attention Span: Using built-in Pomodoro/study timers and tracking how long a student can focus before performance drops.
- Mistakes: The Quiz Engine tracks wrong answers and feeds them into the Mistake Analysis module, identifying weak topics.
- Revision Habits: The Revision Engine uses Spaced Repetition to bring back topics just as the student is about to forget them.
Through this continuous feedback loop, Vortex dynamically reschedules study blocks to ensure maximum retention with minimum suffering.

---

2. NEUROMORPHISM IN VORTEX
=========================================================
How does it use Neuromorphism?
Neuromorphism refers to systems that mimic the neural structure and functioning of the human brain. 

In Vortex, Neuromorphism is applied in two ways:
1. Algorithmic (Brain-Inspired AI): The Recommendation Engine uses "Learning Scores" and "Priority Calculators" that mimic synaptic plasticity. Just like neurons strengthen connections through repeated firing, Vortex strengthens a topic's "priority" when a student struggles, and weakens the priority (pushes it further into the future) when mastery is achieved. It literally mimics human memory decay curves.
2. Interface Design (Neumorphism UI): The frontend is designed with deep, fluid aesthetics (ambient glows, soft glassmorphism, gradient accents). This reduces cognitive load and visual stress, creating a calming environment that contrasts the anxiety typically associated with studying.

3. COMPREHENSIVE FEATURE BREAKDOWN
=========================================================
What every feature does:

A. Dashboard (The Brain Center)
- What it does: Provides a high-level overview of the student's progress.
- Details: Shows active streaks, upcoming deadlines, current overall mastery score, and immediate priority tasks. 
- Unique Element: "AI Study Tip" widget that provides contextual advice based on the student's recent performance (e.g., "You lose focus after 45 minutes of Math, try 25-minute sprints").

B. Dynamic Study Planner (The Core Engine)
- What it does: Generates the daily study schedule.
- Details: Students input subjects, current mastery levels, and exam dates. Clicking "Generate AI Plan" connects to the backend Python models (`priority_calculator.py`, `scheduler.py`). 
- Unique Element: It calculates a "Priority Score" for every topic. If an exam is near and mastery is low, priority spikes. It allocates precise minutes based on available time, utilizing a Drag-and-Drop UI for manual tweaks.

C. Quiz Mode & Mistake Analysis
- What it does: Tests knowledge and finds gaps.
- Details: After completing a topic, the student takes a short quiz.
- Unique Element: It doesn't just grade the student; it feeds the mistakes directly back into the recommendation engine. A failed quiz instantly reschedules that topic for a review the very next day.

D. Progress & Analytics
- What it does: Visualizes the learning journey.
- Details: Uses charts to show attention span trends, mastery growth over time, and subject-wise comparisons.
- Unique Element: Generates downloadable PDF reports (`progress_report.pdf`, `analytics_report.pdf`) that students can review.

E. The Pomodoro & Attention Tracker
- What it does: Manages immediate focus time.
- Details: Integrated directly into the sidebar with a visual SVG ring timer (Focus, Short Break, Long Break).
- Unique Element: The tracker records completion rates. If a student frequently pauses during a 50-minute block, the AI learns this and automatically suggests 25-minute Pomodoros for future sessions.

4. THE UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION (USP)
=========================================================
What makes Vortex truly unique?
Most study planners are "Dumb Calendars." You tell them what to do, and if you miss a day, the whole plan falls apart, causing guilt and stress. 

Vortex is a "Living Schedule."
If a student is tired and studies for only 20 minutes instead of 60, Vortex doesn't send a guilt-inducing notification. Instead, the backend (`recommendation_engine.py`) recalculates the learning curves, shifts the missed 40 minutes across the upcoming week seamlessly, and prioritizes the most critical topics. 

It is the literal embodiment of "Students solving their own suffering through technology." It removes the guilt of falling behind and replaces it with an ever-adapting path forward.
